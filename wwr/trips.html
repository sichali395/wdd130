Songwe River Rapids

Full Day Adventure

Experience the thrill of Class III-IV rapids on the stunning Songwe River. This full-day adventure takes you through breathtaking gorges and lush landscapes, with a riverside lunch included.

Duration: 6-7 hours

Duration: 7-8 hours
